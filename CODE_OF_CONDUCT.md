# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Failure Intelligence Engine project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone — regardless of age, background, experience level, nationality, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Behavior that makes this community great:**

- Being kind and respectful in all interactions
- Giving constructive feedback and accepting it gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Helping newcomers get started without judgment

**Behavior that is not acceptable:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Deliberately hostile or disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other official communication channels.

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it by opening a private issue or emailing the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
